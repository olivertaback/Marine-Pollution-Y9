Introduction
Marine Pollution is a huge problem. Marine pollution is important to bring awareness to because of the millions of animals that are affected by pollution are suffering. In the picture below you can see the amount of garbage floating around this turtle. If awareness is brought to this topic people will know to be careful with where they put their garbage and can save millions of turtles, as well as other marine animals. According to the World Wildlife Foundation, over 100,000 marine animals are killed by pollution. Although, not just the ones found dead are affected, it is estimated that 81/123(about 66% or 2/3) animals in the ocean suffer because of marine pollution.
